The Old Camp is a long-abandoned encampment left behind by fallen warriors and forgotten expeditions. Broken structures and scattered remnants hint at a violent end, as the site is now claimed by the **Possessed Armor Inquisitor**, a relentless guardian bound to the echoes of past conflicts. Few who approach leave without drawing its attention.
## Overview
- Generates naturally in meadow.
- Frequently inhabited by [Possessed Armor](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/entities/possessed_armor).

## Loot
- Possessed Armor Fragments
- Cursed Core
- Iron Nugget
- Rotten Flesh
- Bone
- Cobweb

## Dangers
- Undead enemies may ambush the player.
- Serve as spawn location for elite mobs.
