A ring forged in the depths, blessed to deflect the fury of the beasts of the underworld.

## Stats
- Reduces damage received from **Nether creatures** by **20%**.

## Obtaining
- Can be found within chests in **Scorched Nests** in the **Nether**.

## Crafting
- None.

## Usage
- Can be equipped on a Curio Slot: Ring.

## Advancements
- `Relic of the Forge God`.
