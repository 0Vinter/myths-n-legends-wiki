The **Stone Sanctum Table** is used to craft the **Gargoyle Effigy** to summon a [Gargoyle](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/entities/gargoyle).

## Obtaining

### Crafting
- The **Stone Sanctum Table** can be crafted using chiseled tuff, chiseled tuff bricks and a cursed core.
> Note: For **1.20.1** the recipe uses deepslate blocks.

<CraftingRecipe
    slots={[
        'minecraft:chiseled_tuff_bricks',

        'mythsandlegends:cursed_core',
        '',
        'minecraft:chiseled_tuff_bricks',
        'minecraft:chiseled_tuff_bricks',
        'minecraft:chiseled_tuff_bricks'
    ]}
    result="mythsandlegends:stone_sanctum_table"
