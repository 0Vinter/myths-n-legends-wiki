A relic of shadow and silence, it channels the void into devastating orbs of pure darkness.

## Obtaining
- Defeating **The Black Charro**.

## Usage

### Ability

- Right-Click to shoot an **umbral orb**.
- Cooldown: 1.5 seconds.
