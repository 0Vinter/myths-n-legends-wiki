The **Soulbinder Amulet** This dark amulet pulses with unholy energy, forged to summon **The Black Charro** from his eternal vigil.

## Obtaining

### Crafting
- The **Soulbinder Amulet** can be crafted using bones, gold ingots, ethereal flame and blaze powder.

## Usage

- To activate the altar and begin the summoning ritual, players must possess a **Soulbinder Amulet**. Once the conditions are met, the altar initiates a dark ceremony that calls forth **The Black Charro**.
