The **Possessed Armor Archer** is a ranged variant found in the Ancient Vestiges of the **Myths & Legends Mod**. This spectral entity animates an empty suit of armor, wielding weapon with precision and purpose to protect its territory. Its chilling presence and relentless combat style make it a formidable foe for players exploring these ancient structures.

## Stats
- **Health**: 20 HP
- **Damage**: 2 DP
- **Armor**: 4 AP

## Variants
- [Knight](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/myths-n-legends/docs/entities/possessed_armor)
- [Inquisitor](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/myths-n-legends/docs/entities/possessed_armor_inquisitor)

## Unique Features
- **Immunity:** The Possessed Armor is completely immune to knockback.

## Location
- The **Possessed Armor Archer** exclusively spawns within **Ancient Vestiges Structure**, acting as a guardian of those forgotten places. its purpose is to defend the treasures and secrets hidden within.

## Loot
- `Iron Nugget`
- `Possessed Armor Fragment`
- `Cursed Core`
- `Blue Wool`

## Advancements
**Possessed Armor Archer** contributes to the `Cursed Power`, `Bound Soul Ingot` and `Bound by Darkness`
