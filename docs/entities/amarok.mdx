The **Amarok** is a colossal, predatory wolf born from ancient legends of the frozen wilds. Towering over common beasts, this fearsome creature stalks the tundra as an apex hunter, driven by hunger and primal fury. Its glowing eyes and frost-laden breath are often the last warning travelers receive before the hunt begins.
## Stats
- **Health**: 100 HP
- **Damage**: 8 Pts
- **Armor**: 4 Pts

## Abilities

### Melee Attacks
- The **Gargoyle** uses its immense strength to deliver devastating melee strikes, punishing players who get too close. Each melee attack has a **15% chance to apply a petrification effect**, temporarily turning enemies to stone and making them vulnerable.

### Shockwave
- Upon landing a ground-pounding attack, the **Gargoyle** unleashes a **shockwave** that pushes back all entities within its attack range, dealing damage in the process.

## Unique Features
- **Immunity**: Amarok is **completely immune to all forms of magic damage and freezing**.

## Location
- Can be found in **Snowy Biomes** only at Full Moon Phase.

## Loot
- `Amarok Essence`
- `Amarok Hide`

## Advancements
- **Amarok** contributes to the `Howl of the Lonely End` and `Reliquary of the Northern Spirit`.
