The **Lampad** is a mysterious forest dweller wreathed in green flame, carrying a flickering lantern that trails ghostly light through deep woods. Unsettling and elusive, it terrifies unwary wanderers with its sudden appearances.

## Stats
- **Health**: 30 HP
- **Damage**: 6 DP
- **Armor**: 2 AP

## Unique Features
- **Immunity**: immune to fire and lava.
- Can walk over lava.

## Location
- Can be found in **Warped Forests**.

## Loot
- `Ethereal Flame`

## Advancements
- None.
