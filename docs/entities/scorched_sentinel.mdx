The **Scorched Sentinel** is a stone sentinel introduced in the *Myths & Legends Mod*. This fearsome creature stands as a guardian of the night, lying dormant during the day and coming to life only under the cover of darkness. With its unique abilities and near-immunity to damage, the Gargoyle is a formidable adversary that tests the strength of any adventurer.

## Stats
- **Health**: 35 HP
- **Damage**: 6 DP
- **Armor**: 0 AP

## Unique Features
- None.

## Location
- The **Scorched Sentinel** exclusively spawns within **Scorched Nest**.

## Loot
- `Scorched Bone`
- `Scorched Skin`

- None.
