A sinister figure from dark folklore, the **Alp** is a passive yet thieving entity that stalks players from the shadows. Quick and cunning, it snatches items from unsuspecting travelers before vanishing into the night.

## Stats
- **Health**: 10 HP
- **Damage**: 0 AP
- **Armor**: 0 AP

## Unique Features
- **Weakness**: Alp burns in sunlight.
- **Thief**: Can steal items from players.

## Location
- Can be found in **Swamps**.

## Loot
- Items stolen from player.
