The **Possessed Armor Inquisitor** is an elite version of **Possessed Armor** found in the Old Camp of the **Myths & Legends Mod**. This spectral entity animates an empty suit of armor, wielding weapon with precision and purpose to protect its territory. Its chilling presence and relentless combat style make it a formidable foe for players exploring these ancient structures.

## Stats
- **Health**: 120 HP
- **Damage**: 12 AP
- **Armor**: 8 AP

## Unique Features
- **Immunity**: The Possessed Armor Inquisitor is **completely immune to knockback.
- **Stun**: 10% chance to be stun by the player when hit.

## Location
- The **Possessed Armor Inquisitor** exclusively spawns within **Old Camp Structure**.

## Loot
- `Iron Nugget`
- `Possessed Armor Fragment`
- `Cursed Core`

## Advancements
**Possessed Armor Inquisitor** contributes to the `Cursed Power`, `Bound Soul Ingot` and `Bound by Darkness`
