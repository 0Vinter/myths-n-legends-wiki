**Abaddon**, the Burning Tyrant, is a towering elemental forged in the deepest infernos of the Nether. Cloaked in molten chains and scorched armor, it commands searing flames and meteoric wrath to annihilate intruders. Its rage burns eternal, consuming all that stands before it.

## Stats
- **Health**: 25 HP
- **Damage**: 5
- **Armor**: 2.5 Pts

## Abilities

### Melee Attacks
- The **Gargoyle** uses its immense strength to deliver devastating melee strikes, punishing players who get too close. Each melee attack has a **15% chance to apply a petrification effect**, temporarily turning enemies to stone and making them vulnerable.

### Shockwave
- **Infernal Crash:** Slams the ground, erupting waves of fire and magma.

## Unique Features
- **Immunity**: immune to fire and lava.

## Location
- Can be found in **Crimson Forests**.

## Loot
- `Core of Abaddon`

## Advancements
- None.
